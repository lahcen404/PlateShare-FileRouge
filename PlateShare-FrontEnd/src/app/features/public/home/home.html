How PlateShare Works

1. Donors List Surplus

Restaurants, supermarkets, and individuals easily publish their surplus food with details like type, quantity, and expiration.

2. Requesters Find Food

Charities and individuals in need search and filter available listings to find fresh, healthy food in their local area.

3. Connect & Share

Requesters reserve items and coordinate pickup, ensuring good food gets to people who need it, not to the landfill.
